01Venue

This year's camp is hosted by Leeds YPG at Life Centre Events in Bradford. Accommodation is included in the Standard Ticket and will be allocated at Travelodge Bradford Central.

Rooms will be allocated ahead of the day. The aim is for guilders to get to know one another and build relationships during the stay, while keeping allocations fair.

02Getting there

Transport to camp and back should be organised by your local executives. If that isn't possible, contact the district executives at ypgmidlands@gmail.com to discuss your options.

Head to Faith PCG (address sent closer to the date) by 1PM on Thursday afternoon for the smoothest arrival.

Arrive at Leeds train station and arrangements will be made to pick you up. Let your local president know in advance so they can coordinate with the Midlands District Execs.

Head straight to the venue, Life Centre Events, from 5PM onwards. This applies to Thursday-only attendance.

03Pricing

Ticket prices are lower than last year — the District has contributed significantly toward camp costs to keep it affordable and make sure everyone can attend and grow in their relationship with God.

£135Standard Ticket (full camp + accommodation)
£60Ultimate Day Pass
£50Fri + Sat full day, meals included
£25Single day — Friday or Saturday
£10Thursday evening only, from 5PM

Get in touch so the team can suggest ways to support you — via the website, email, social media, or any district executive.

Registration has closed; payments are collected via www.ypgmld.co.uk. Deadline: Sunday 30th June, 12PM. If you haven't registered but still want to join, email the team — spaces are limited but they'll try to accommodate you.

Refunds are a 50/50 split, since the camp carries a liability with the hotel for every registered attendee. Contact the team for more detail.

05Food & dress code

Feedback from last year has been taken on board — local Ghanaian dishes will be served. A detailed menu will be shared closer to the date.

Dress comfortably, but modestly, to represent the Presbyterian Church of Ghana throughout the stay.

For the Gala/YPG Awards Night: tuxedo and evening dress. For Saturday evening, every guilder is asked to wear a touch of white. Don't forget sports gear for sports day — a packing list follows soon.

06Other logistics

Yes — everyone is welcome, including brothers and sisters from the North and South London Districts.

Photos and videos will be taken throughout camp, including on social media and livestreams. If you'd prefer not to be included, email ypgmidlands@gmail.com with your full name stating so. No response is taken as consent to be included.
